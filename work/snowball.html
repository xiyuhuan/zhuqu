Snowball

Year
2025
industry
Agency

Influence at scale, made authentic.

(Scroll down)

Brands need massive, credible visibility when they launch a product. Traditional influence agencies feel heavy, expensive, and built around faces that read as too sponsored. Snowball flips the model: dozens of micro-creators activated at once, for a snowball effect across social feeds. The challenge was clear: prove the project is solid and structured, without ever looking corporate, cold, or out of touch with the codes brands and creators live by.

Brand Appart partnered with Snowball to define its positioning and build a brand identity that lands in seconds. We had to clarify the promise, energize the look, and reassure decision-makers at the same time. The identity carries the snowball idea — collective amplification — while staying young, sharp, and instantly legible. Distinctive enough to stop the scroll, structured enough to close the sale.

Snowball doesn't chase the loudest influencers. Snowball multiplies authentic voices — turning a single launch into an impression of omnipresence, and visibility into trust.
